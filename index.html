About Me

I am currently a second year master student majoring in Robotics at Delft University of Technology. My research interests lie at the intersection of Reinforcement Learning and Neuromorphic Computing. I am particularly interested in exploring how bio-inspired algorithms can be applied to bio-inspired robots.

Currently, I am working with Dr.Cosimo Della Santina on Bio-inspired Control for Soft Snake Robots, where we are investigating the combination of bio-inspired STDP learning, reservoir computing, and Reinforcement Learning for snake locomotion. I am applying for PhD positions starting in Fall 2026.
